Curious little birds

Kiwis are nocturnal and snuffle about the beach at night. When you track an element with Kiwi Runner, Kiwi Runner does exactly the same. Well... not exactly.

Kiwi runner will snuffle the internet once a day (just after midnight, Pacific time), visit your site in the dead of night, and and take note of how the data in the element you're tracking has changed (if at all). It'll then save that data to our database and you'll have access to it whenever you like.

Kiwis are social! Feel free to share your kiwi findings with your friends! If they're popular, you might raise a top-trending kiwi! You'll have to make an account for this... click here to get started.

Data-driven dodos

In the course of your data-detecting endeavours, you'll probably find tons of cool information. Kiwi runner comes with a light-weight analysis platform for you to view your kiwis, plot them against each other, and compare them with other pieces of data on the internet.

Kiwi runner uses the popular D3 analytics library allowing you to create awesome diagrams right here online, with no fancy software. Head over to the Data Dojo to see your kiwis, top-trending kiwis, and start building some awesome charts.
